# HOM Mobile Assistant (HOMMA) - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Welcome to the **HOM Mobile Assistant (HOMMA)**. This Progressive Web Application (PWA) is designed to help House of Mercy (HOM) Program Participants and House Leaders/Staff manage their tasks, communicate efficiently, and submit required documentation directly from their mobile devices.

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) provides a comprehensive guide to using the application.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the App
HOMMA is a Progressive Web App (PWA). This means you can access it via a web browser on your phone and install it to your home screen like a native app.
1. Open your mobile browser and navigate to the provided HOMMA URL.
2. Follow your device's prompts to "Add to Home Screen" for easy access.

### Theme Preferences
You can toggle between Light and Dark mode using the sun/moon icon located in the top-right corner of the application headers.

---

## 2. Login Process

Upon launching the app, you will see a daily inspirational gospel verse. Below it, choose your user role to log in.

### A. Program Participant Login
1. Tap on the **Program Participant Login** option.
2. Enter your HOM-provided email address.
3. Enter your password. *(If you do not have a password, request one from your Care Navigator).*
4. Tap **Enter** to access your dashboard.

### B. House Leader / Staff Login
1. Tap on the **House Leader Login** option.
2. Select the specific House you manage from the dropdown menu.
3. Enter your assigned **Staff ID**.
4. (Optional) Check "Remember this device" to save your login session.
5. Tap **Enter** to access the staff dashboard.

---

## 3. House Leader / Staff Functionality

Once logged in, House Leaders have access to a comprehensive dashboard to manage their house and residents.

### A. Home Dashboard
The dashboard provides a quick overview of important metrics and tools:
- **House KPIs:** View statistics like House Meetings (last 7 days), and Present/Absent metrics for the last 30 days.
- **Alerts:** View critical status alerts.
- **Manage House Leader Tasks:** Tap to view and complete daily/weekly assigned tasks.
- **Pending New Arrivals:** See incoming residents, their age, SO Level, and projected arrival date.
- **Transportation Requests:** Expand this section to view open or scheduled transportation requests for residents.
- **HOM Bulletin Board:** View the latest announcements.

### B. Managing Residents
- **Active Participants List:** A list of all current residents in your house.
- **Resident Details:** Tap on a resident's photo to view detailed information (DOB, SO Level, CCO contact info, financial status, etc.) or submit specific forms on their behalf.
- **Resident Actions:** When tapping a resident, you can add Observation Reports, Participant 1-on-1s, or submit a Resident Update.

### C. Submitting Expense Receipts
Staff can submit digital records of expenses and attach receipts directly to Quickbase.
1. From the Resident Detail or specific menu, tap **Submit Expense Receipt**.
2. **Vendor:** Type (minimum 2 characters) and select the vendor from the dropdown.
3. **Details:** Enter the Expense Date, Quantity, and Amount ($).
4. **Grant Info:** Indicate if it's a grant expense. If yes, select the specific grant type.
5. **Description:** Provide a detailed reason for the purchase.
6. **Capture Receipt:** Tap to either take a photo with your device's camera or upload a PDF/Image of the receipt.
7. Tap **Submit Expense Receipt**.

### D. Submitting Feedback & Requests
Use the "Submit Prayer Request or Feedback" card on the home dashboard to communicate with HOM Staff.
1. Select the specific Staff Member you wish to contact.
2. Choose a Request Type (Prayer Request, Feedback, Maintenance Request, Support Needed, Suggestion, Concern/Issue).
3. Enter the details of your message.
4. Tap **Submit Request**.

---

## 4. Program Participant Functionality

Participants use the app to view their status, request services, and communicate with staff.

### A. Participant Dashboard
Upon logging in, you will land on your Participant Details page.
- **Personal Info:** View your House Leader, Care Navigator, Program Manager, and Program Director contact details. Tap the phone numbers to initiate a call.
- **Status & Financials:** View your participant status, bed start date, room number, fees due, past due fees, and active pay plans.
- **HOM Bulletin Board:** View recent global announcements from HOM.

### B. Submitting Forms and Requests
From your dashboard, use the colored buttons to perform various tasks:
- **Add Transport Request:** Submit a request for transportation. You will need to specify the purpose, date, time, pickup location, and destination.
- **Register for a Meeting/Class:** View upcoming classes and training sessions and register to attend.
- **Go to Message Center:** Send direct messages or prayer requests to specific HOM staff members. If you get a new job or have life changes, report them here.
- **Pay Program Fees or Give/Tithe:** Opens a secure external link to process payments or donations.

### C. Logging Out
To ensure security, always log out when you are finished.
- Tap **Return to Login Page** or **Sign Out/Exit App** from your dashboard to securely log out.

---

## 5. Troubleshooting & Support
- **Error Messages:** If you encounter an error (e.g., during login or form submission), ensure you have a stable internet connection.
- **Forgotten Passwords:** Participants should contact their Care Navigator. Staff should contact IT support.
- **App Updates:** Since HOMMA is a PWA, you will be prompted to reload the app when a new version is available. Always accept these updates to ensure optimal performance.
