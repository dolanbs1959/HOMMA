# HOM Mobile Assistant (HOMMA) - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Welcome to the **HOM Mobile Assistant (HOMMA)**. This Progressive Web Application (PWA) is designed to help House of Mercy (HOM) Program Participants and House Leaders/Staff manage their tasks, communicate efficiently, and submit required documentation directly from their mobile devices.

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) provides a comprehensive, step-by-step guide to using the application.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the App
HOMMA is a Progressive Web App (PWA). This means you can access it via a web browser on your phone and install it to your home screen like a native app.
1. Open your mobile browser and navigate to the provided HOMMA URL.
2. Follow your device's prompts to "Add to Home Screen" for easy access.

---

## 2. Login Process

Upon launching the app, you will see a daily inspirational gospel verse. Below it, choose your user role to log in.

### A. Program Participant Login
1. Tap on the **Program Participant Login** option.
2. Enter your HOM-provided email address.
3. Enter your password. *(If you do not have a password, request one from your Care Navigator).*
4. Tap **Enter** to access your dashboard.

### B. House Leader Login
1. Tap on the **House Leader Login** option.
2. Select the specific House you manage from the dropdown menu.
3. Enter your assigned **Staff ID**.
4. (Optional) Check "Remember this device" to save your login session.
5. Tap **Enter** to access the staff dashboard.

---

## 3. House Leader Functionality

Once logged in, House Leaders have access to a comprehensive dashboard to manage their house and residents.

### A. Home Dashboard
The dashboard provides a quick overview of important metrics and tools:
- **House KPIs:** View statistics like House Meetings (last 7 days), and Present/Absent metrics for the last 30 days.
- **Alerts:** View critical status alerts.
- **Manage House Leader Tasks:** Tap to view and complete daily/weekly assigned tasks.
- **Active Participants:** Listing of all participants residing in the selected house.
- **Pending New Arrivals:** See incoming residents, their age, SO Level, and projected arrival date.
- **Transportation Requests:** Expand this section to view open or scheduled transportation requests for residents.
- **Feedback & Prayer Requests:** Used to communicate with staff.
- **HOM Bulletin Board:** View the latest announcements.

### B. Managing House Leader Tasks
Tap **Manage House Leader Tasks** from the Home Dashboard.
1. **View Tasks:** You will see a list of assigned tasks, showing status, frequency, and priority. If a "1 on 1" task is due, it will specify how many are needed.
2. **Add Weekly House Meeting:**
   - Tap **Add Weekly House Meeting**.
   - Enter an **Activity Description** in the text area detailing what was discussed.
   - For each resident listed, select their **Attendance Status** (Present, Excused Absence, Unexcused Absence, Excused Tardy, Unexcused Tardy).
   - If a resident was absent or tardy, enter **Comments** providing the reason.
   - Tap **Add Weekly House Meeting** at the bottom to submit. Once submitted, you can tap **Update Attendance Records** if any corrections are needed prior to leaving the page.
   - You will see a confirmation pop-up once records are recorded successfully. Tap **OK** to return.
3. Tap **Back/Start Over** to return to the Home Dashboard.

### C. Managing Residents
From the Home Dashboard, you can select an active participant or a pending arrival to perform actions.
Tap on a resident's photo or name to open the **Resident Actions** menu. You can perform the following:

#### 1. Resident Vitals
Tap **Resident Vitals** to view detailed participant information:
- View DOB, Age, Bed Start Date, SO Level, Room/Bed assignment.
- View Care Navigator, Program Manager, Program Director, and CCO contact details (Tap a number to initiate a call).
- View Financial Status, Fees Due, and Past Due Fees.

#### 2. Add Observation Report
Used to log specific behaviors or incidents.
- Tap **Add Observation Report**.
- Select one or more **Observation Types** (e.g., Behavior, Rule Violation).
- Select the **Observation Tone** (e.g., Positive, Negative, Neutral).
- Select the **Observation Class**.
- Type a detailed **Observation Description**.
- **Transportation Details (If applicable):** Enter Departure/Return dates and times, Location/Address, County, and Emergency Contact details.
- Select Yes/No for **CCO Approved**, **SOTP Therapist Approved**, **Safety Plan Required**, and **Attend Required Meeting**.
- Tap **Submit Report**.

#### 3. Add Resident Update
Used to update the participant's status or room assignment.
- Tap **Add Resident Update**.
- Select the **Occupancy Status** (e.g., Active, Move, Discharged).
- Select the **Move Date**.
- If the status is related to moving, select the **New Room #** and **New Bed #**.
- Enter any **Notes**.
- Tap **Submit Update**.

#### 4. Add Participant 1-on-1 (Participant Reviews)
Used to log required check-in meetings with the participant.
- Tap **Add Participant 1-on-1**.
- Verify or update the participant's phone number and email address.
- Select Yes/No for current statuses: **On Supervision**, **In SOTP**, **In Drug/Alcohol Treatment**, and **In Mental Health Counseling**.
- Verify or update the **CCO Contact Information** (Name, Phone, Mobile, Email).
- Add detailed **Meeting Notes**, including comments on any recent observations.
- Enter the **Meeting Administrator**'s name.
- Select **How Administered** (e.g., Live, Phone).
- Tap **Submit Review**.

#### 5. Class Registration
Used to register the participant for required training or classes.
- Tap **Class Registration**.
- A list of **Available Training Modules** will appear.
- Tap the module you wish to register them for, then tap **Select a Meeting/Class**.
- A list of specific classes for that module will display (including date, description, and # of registered).
- Tap the specific class, then tap **Register for Class**.

#### 6. Add Transport Request
Used to request a ride for the participant.
- Tap **Add Transport Request**.
- Select the **Purpose** (e.g., Prison Pick-up, Dept Of Corrections CCO).
- Select the **Date Requested** and **Time Requested**.
- Select the **Pick-up Location** and **Destination Location** from the dropdown lists.
- Enter detailed **Transportation Notes**.
- Tap **Submit Request**.

### D. Submitting Feedback & Requests
Use the "Submit Prayer Request or Feedback" card on the home dashboard to communicate with HOM Staff.
1. Select the specific Staff Member you wish to contact from the dropdown.
2. Choose a **Request Type** (Prayer Request, Feedback, Maintenance Request, Support Needed, Suggestion, Concern/Issue).
3. Enter the details of your message in the text area.
4. Tap **Submit Request**. You will see a success message once it goes through.

## 4. Program Participant Functionality

Participants use the app to view their status, request services, and communicate with staff.

### A. Participant Dashboard
Upon logging in, you will land directly on your Participant Details page.
- **Personal Info:** View your House Leader, Care Navigator, Program Manager, and Program Director contact details. Tap the phone numbers to initiate a call.
- **Status & Financials:** View your participant status, bed start date, room number, fees due, past due fees, and active pay plans.
- **HOM Bulletin Board:** View recent global announcements from HOM.

### B. Submitting Forms and Requests
From your dashboard, scroll down to use the colored buttons to perform various tasks:

#### 1. Add Transport Request
- Tap **Add Transport Request**.
- Select the **Purpose** of the trip.
- Choose the **Date** and **Time** you need the ride.
- Select your **Pick-up Location** and your **Destination Location**.
- Provide any **Transportation Notes** the driver might need to know.
- Tap **Submit Request**.

#### 2. Register for a Meeting/Class
- Tap **Register for a Meeting/Class**.
- Select a **Training Module** from the list, then tap **Select a Meeting/Class**.
- Choose the specific class date/time you wish to attend, then tap **Register for Class**.

#### 3. Go to Message Center
Use this to send direct messages to HOM staff regarding job changes, life updates, or prayer requests.
- Tap **Go to Message Center**.
- Select the **Staff Member** you want to message (e.g., select Michael Lovrick for prayer requests).
- Enter your message in the **Comment** box. Be as detailed as possible.
- Tap **Submit Message**.

#### 4. Pay Program Fees or Give/Tithe
- Tap **Pay Program Fees or Give/Tithe**.
- This will open a secure external window where you can process your payments or make a donation.

### C. Logging Out
To ensure security, always log out when you are finished.
- Tap **Return to Login Page** or **Sign Out/Exit App** from your dashboard to securely log out.

---

## 5. Troubleshooting & Support
- **Error Messages:** If you encounter an error (e.g., during login or form submission), ensure you have a stable internet connection.
- **Forgotten Passwords:** Participants should contact their Care Navigator. Staff should contact IT support.
- **App Updates:** Since HOMMA is a PWA, you will be prompted to reload the app when a new version is available. Always accept these updates to ensure optimal performance.
